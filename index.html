Welcome!

I am an Assistant Professor at the Yale University Economics Department.


I am also a Research Fellow at the Institute for Fiscal Studies, a Faculty Research Fellow at the National Bureau of Economic Research, a Research Affiliate at the Economic and Social Research Insitute and a Research Fellow at DIW Berlin.


I previously studied economics at Trinity College Dublin, and did my PhD at University College London. I worked at the Institute for Fiscal Studies before and during my PhD.
